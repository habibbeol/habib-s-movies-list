7. The Lord of the Rings :Three movies together.

An epic fantasy adventure thrilogy that plunges into a fictional world, following a young man who inherits a powerful ring and embarks on a perilous journey to destroy it. Guided by a great wizard, he soon learns of the immense dark energy it holds, and that it must be destroyed on a certain mountain to save all nations from the dominance of evil. As he marches towards this destination, the evil nations, sensing his purpose, unleash hell on earth to hunt him down and end his quest .
